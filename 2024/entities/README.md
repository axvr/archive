Experiments in entities being living things within a system rather than
creating a system of CRUD operations.  We keep trying to model living processes
as mechanical/dead operations ones.  Let's bring them to life and make them
active participants in the system.

This was an evolution of my [prior experiment into a message-passing
data-driven workflow orchestrator][catalyst] (also known as Catalyst).

I really like this concept and wish to explore it further.
