Unlike many other hosted languages (such as Clojure and F\#) that have
[mutualistic][] relationships with their hosts, Catalyst will have
a [commensalistic][] relationship with its, as it benefits from the host, but
the host gets nothing in return.  This is due to the isolationary goals of
Catalyst.

[mutualistic]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mutualism_(biology)
[commensalistic]: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commensalism
